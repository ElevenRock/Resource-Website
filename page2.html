Preparing your paper

Start by selecting paper items you want to recycle and tear them into small pieces. The quality of the paper you use will affect the strength of the recycled paper.


You can either shred the paper using a shredder for convenience or rip it by hand.


Suitable materials range from

  • Notebook pages
  • Old homework
  • Brainstorming materials
  • Essays
  • Unwanted drawings and sketches
  • Old assignments and journals
  • Junk mail, newspapers, etc.

Place the torn or shredded paper into a container and submerge it in water to soak. Allow it to soak for at least 12 hours to soften adequately, which will help in turning it into a pulp. The longer the paper is soaked, the softer it will become
