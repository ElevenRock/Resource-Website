Drying Area

  1. Use two clothespins per sheet to gently pull apart and hang the sheets on a drying rack, handling them carefully as the wet pulp is fragile.

  2. Allow the sheets to dry overnight and check them the next morning.

  3. For flattening the dried paper, leave it under a book press or a stack of books for a day or two to ensure it is completely flat.
